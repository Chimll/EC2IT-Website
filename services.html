Core Services

What we do.

01

End User Helpdesk

Fast, friendly support for your entire team — remote or on-site. Our engineers pick up the phone and resolve issues, not log tickets for someone else to deal with later.

  • Remote support via secure desktop session
  • On-site visits same or next day
  • 90-minute SLA guaranteed
  • No tiered scripting — direct engineer access
  • Monthly usage reporting
02

24/7 Systems Monitoring

We monitor your servers, network and critical systems around the clock, automatically alerting and responding to issues before they affect your business.

  • Server, network & endpoint monitoring
  • Automated alerting and escalation
  • Proactive patch management
  • Performance reporting and capacity planning
  • Bank holiday and weekend coverage
03

Security & Backups

Enterprise-grade protection for your data, systems and people. From endpoint security to disaster recovery, we make sure your business is resilient and compliant.

  • Endpoint detection & response (EDR)
  • Email filtering and anti-phishing
  • Encrypted cloud backup with tested restores
  • Disaster recovery planning and documentation
  • Cyber Essentials guidance and support
04

Office 365 & Cloud

Seamless Microsoft 365 migration, day-to-day management and strategic optimisation. We ensure your team gets the most out of the tools they use every day.

  • M365 migration and tenant setup
  • Exchange Online and Teams configuration
  • SharePoint and OneDrive management
  • Azure Virtual Desktop (AVD)
  • Licence optimisation and cost control
05

Purchasing & Licensing

Right hardware, right software, best price — managed from procurement to deployment. We handle every aspect of your technology purchasing so you don't have to.

  • Hardware sourcing (laptops, servers, networking)
  • Software licensing management
  • Vendor negotiation and volume discounts
  • Warranty and asset management
  • End-of-life planning and refresh scheduling
06

IT Project Delivery

Office moves, server migrations, infrastructure upgrades — planned, managed and delivered on time with minimal disruption to your team.

  • Office relocations and fit-outs
  • Server room builds and upgrades
  • Network infrastructure design and install
  • System migrations (on-prem to cloud)
  • Post-project documentation and handover
07

Service Integration

We work as an extension of your internal IT team or as your fully outsourced IT department — whichever model works best for your business.

  • Flexible co-managed IT models
  • Handoff and escalation workflows
  • On-demand specialist resource
  • IT strategy and roadmap planning
  • Vendor management on your behalf
08

IT Management & Reporting

Strategic oversight, asset tracking and transparent monthly reporting. You always know exactly what's happening with your IT, and where your budget is going.

  • Monthly IT health reports
  • Asset inventory and lifecycle tracking
  • Technology budget planning
  • Dedicated account manager
  • Annual IT strategy review
09

Comprehensive IT Infrastructure

Networking, telephony, printing, mobile devices — everything beyond the screen. We manage the full estate so nothing falls through the cracks.

  • LAN/WAN and Wi-Fi design and support
  • VOIP telephony and call management
  • Printer and MFD fleet management
  • Mobile device management (MDM)
  • Firewall and router management
