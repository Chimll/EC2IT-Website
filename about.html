About EC2IT

We're a team of London-based IT engineers who believe that great IT support should be straightforward, honest and genuinely proactive — not a last resort.

Our Story

Where we
came from.

EC2IT was founded in 2009 in the heart of the City of London, with a simple ambition: to give small and medium-sized businesses access to the kind of IT support that was previously only available to large enterprises.

In the years since, we've grown into one of London's most trusted IT partners — supporting over 500 users across sectors including finance, architecture, law, energy and technology.

What's never changed is the way we work. No call centres. No scripted first-line support. No bouncing between tiers. When you call EC2IT, you speak to an engineer — someone who knows your systems, understands your business and can actually fix your problem.

Our team is based in London and we're proud to offer on-site visits across the city, same or next day. We're part of the business communities we serve, and we take that responsibility seriously.
